A brief History About Us:

BINGEWATCH is an Indian technology and service provider headquartered in Bengaluru, India, founded in 2010. The company's primary business is its subscription-based streaming services which offers online streaming of a library of films and television series, including those produced in-house. It has become available worldwide in the current scenario. The company now has offices in France, United States, United Kingdom, Brazil, the Netherlands, Japan, and South Korea.

Bingewatch over the years:

BINGEWATCH was started with a small investment and a total of 50 employees working under one roof. It later grew and expanded giving work to more than thousands of people in each country. In its first year BINGEWATCH had a total subscription of only 5000 in India but as of now it has grown to be very popular with a total paid subscription of 12 million across the world.
