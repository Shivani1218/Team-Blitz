Here are the answers to some frequently asked questions:

1) How to stop someone from using your account?

If someone is using your account without permission, do the following to prevent further use: 1) Change your Bingewatch password. 2) Sign out of all devices connected to your account. If you are unable to reset your password, or if you continue seeing suspicious account activity after completing the steps above,please contact us.

2) How to update password?

Sign in to the Bingewatch website to change your password.
You may be required to confirm your current payment information to make the change. If you can't sign in with your current password, send yourself a password reset email. If you've forgotten the email address associated with your account, go to the payment page and verify. If you see an error message when you attempt to update your password, please contact us..

3) How to connect to Email?

To connect to Email, visit About us page and email us in the provided address

How to create and edit profiles?

Profiles allow different members of your household to have their own personalized Bingewatch experience. You can have up to four profiles within a single Bingewatch account. To create a profile, 1) Go to settings 2) Select add profile 3) Name the profile and continue streaming.

How to download TV shows and movies?

Open the Netflix app and select a TV show or movie. Not all titles are available for download. 1) From the description page, tap Downloads . Downloads will appear next to each available episode. 2) To save on data usage, download over Wi-Fi.

6) How to review payment history?

Sign in to your Account and select Billing details to see your Bingewatch plan and billing history.
